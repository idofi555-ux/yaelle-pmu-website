What is Lip Blushing?

Lip blushing is a semi-permanent cosmetic tattoo that enhances your natural lip color and shape. Using specialized techniques, pigment is deposited into the lips to create a soft, natural-looking tint that lasts for years.

Perfect For

  • Pale or uneven lip color
  • Asymmetrical lip shape
  • Undefined lip borders
  • Lips that have lost color with age
  • Those wanting low-maintenance beauty

Benefits

  • Enhanced natural lip color
  • More defined lip shape
  • Corrected asymmetry
  • Youthful, fresh appearance
  • No daily lipstick needed

Aftercare

  • Keep lips moisturized
  • Avoid spicy foods for 1 week
  • No kissing for 7-10 days
  • Apply healing balm regularly
  • Avoid sun exposure during healing

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does lip blushing last?

Lip blushing typically lasts 2-3 years. The longevity depends on factors like sun exposure, skincare routine, and lifestyle. Annual touch-ups are recommended to maintain the color intensity.

Is lip blushing painful?

A topical numbing cream is applied before and during the procedure. Most clients describe the sensation as mildly uncomfortable but very manageable. The lips may feel slightly swollen afterward.

What colors are available for lip blushing?

We offer a range of natural shades from nude pink to berry tones. During your consultation, we'll select a color that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural lip color.

How long is the healing process?

Initial healing takes about 7-10 days. Your lips will appear darker initially and then lighten by 30-50% as they heal. The final color settles in after about 4-6 weeks.
