What is Brow Lamination?

Brow lamination is like a perm for your eyebrows. It relaxes and redirects the brow hairs to create a fuller, more uniform look that stays in place for weeks.

Perfect For

  • Unruly or curly brow hairs
  • Thin or sparse brows
  • Brows that won't stay in place
  • Those wanting a fuller look
  • Low-maintenance styling

The Process

  • Brow shaping and mapping
  • Lifting solution applied
  • Hairs brushed into position
  • Setting solution applied
  • Optional tint for definition

Benefits

  • Non-permanent treatment
  • Fuller-looking brows
  • Covers gaps and sparse areas
  • No daily styling needed
  • Perfect base for microblading
